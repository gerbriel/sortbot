How it works

  1. Upload — drag in the day's shoot; photos compress and upload resumably, even on slow connections.
  2. Group — auto-group by photos-per-item, or rapid-fire pick mode; drag onto categories with presets that pre-fill shipping and SEO.
  3. Describe — dictate per listing; fields, measurements, and the description write themselves.
  4. Export — download the Shopify CSV (or hand it to your VA) and import. Done before lunch.
