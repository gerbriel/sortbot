- Brand names (Supreme, BAPE, Carhartt, etc.)

- Subcultures (punk, skate, grunge, hip-hop)
- Materials (selvage, gore-tex, duck canvas)

### 2. Knowledge Retrieval
When pattern matches, retrieves 2-3 relevant contextual sentences from knowledge base:
- Brand heritage & founding stories
- Cultural significance & adoption

### 3. Smart Selection
- Randomly selects 2-3 additions (prevents repetition)
- Prioritizes most specific matches (band name > genre, brand > category)
- Combines multiple contexts when appropriate
